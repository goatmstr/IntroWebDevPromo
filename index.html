The New York Knicks

The New York Knicks are a basketball team in the NBA with a very long history, being one of the first professional basketball teams ever. It's home arena is the iconic Madison Square Garden. Although the team has had its ups and downs in recent years, its fans are among the most dedicated and loyal fans in the NBA. The Knicks have been playing especially well recently thanks to the efforts of Jalen Brunson who was selected as an All Star for the first time. Be sure to support the Knicks by going to some home games at the garden!
